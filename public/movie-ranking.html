Movie Reviews

based on someone who enjoys movies

  1. Iron Man 2008

    With its first self-financed production, Marvel has produced one of the best superhero movies ever made, pure popcorn pleasure for its special effects, its story, its villain, and its hero.

  2. The Incredible Hulk 2008

    What Hulk smash most? Hulk smash all hope of interesting time in cinema.

  3. Iron Man 2 2010

    If you're just going to see men in robot suits blow each other up while classic rock blares on the soundtrack, Iron Man 2 will do just fine...

  4. Thor 2011

    Thor changed everything. Thor gave us a universe.

  5. Captain America: The First Avenger 2011

    The Captain isn't perfect, but he's the equal of Thor and loads better than the Green Lantern: he's the summer's pre-eminent superhero.

  6. The Avengers 2012

    After two-and-a-half hours of world-threatening chaos and world-saving derring-do, you leave the theater satisfied, but without so much as a single memorable image (or idea) lingering in your mind.
